## Company Description

### Main Statement
Welcome to RUS Chartered Accountants. We are a reputable and long established firm of Chartered Accountants, Registered Auditors and Tax Advisors based in Hall Green, West Midlands, Birmingham. We are authorized by the Institute of Chartered Accountants in England & Wales to carry out Accountancy, Taxation & Audit engagements.

### History & Experience
RUS Chartered Accountants have been established for over 20 years, serving the business community to achieve objectives of growth and prosperity through advice and services.

### Team
We have a large team of competent staff consisting of qualified Chartered Accountants through to Tax specialists to provide all round efficient and professional services needed in a changing and complex financial environment.

### Client Base
Here at RUS Chartered Accountants, we have a vast and diverse client base ranging from the medical professionals and IT contractors to retail and manufacturing businesses throughout the United Kingdom.

### Service Promise
Whatever your needs, RUS Chartered Accountants are here to provide a high quality bespoke service at a competitive fee regardless of your geographical locality within the United Kingdom.
